Two Layer Tic-Tac-Toe

Welcome to Two Layer Tic-Tac-Toe!
Two Layer Tic-Tac-toe is mechanically the same as normal tic-tac-toe, however there are some new rules.
  • players can take spaces away from one another. A space can only be locked if you take it twice.
  • If there is a letter on the space, whether once or twice, the space is considered held by that player. A player can win the game with connected single-layer captures.
  • A player may not move on the same space his or her opponent took on the previous turn.
Hopefully the rules were simple, enjoy the game!
