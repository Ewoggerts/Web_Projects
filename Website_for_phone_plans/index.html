Disjunction Wireless


There are 3 phone plans:
1) Basic - $19.99 base price. Customers receive 1GB (1000 megabytes) of data and are charged 10 cents for each MB they use over the 1000.

2) Comprehensive - $24.99 base price. Customers receive 4GB of data (4000 megabytes) and are charged 25 cents for each MB they use over the 4000.

3) Sucker - $4.99 base price. Customers pay 2 cents per megabyte.

4) Unlimited - $49.99 base price. Customers would not be charged for usage.
